A Brief History

The University of Arkansas at Little Rock (UALR) is a public research university in Little Rock, Arkansas made in 1927. its campus has 250 acres with more than 56 buildings.

Academics

  • College of Humanities, Arts, Social Sciences, and Education

  • William H. Bowen School of Law
